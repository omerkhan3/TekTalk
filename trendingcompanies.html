Snap Inc. Files for $25 Billion IPO

By: Omer Khan | 12/10/16

Snap Inc, formerly known as Snapchat, has filed paperwork for an initial public offering that may value as high $25 billion. This would be one of the biggest stock debuts in recent years. The Wall Street Journal has reported that the valuation could be $25 billion or more. A debut of this magnitude could be great for the IPO market, which has struggled recently. Currently there are a small batch of highly valued and closely watched venture-backed companies, and Snap will be the first to test the public markets. A successful debut may prompt others in this group such as Uber and Airbnb to follow suit.
